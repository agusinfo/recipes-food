Ingredients of Soto Daging (Beef Soup with coconut milk and turmeric)

  1. Prepare 300 g of beef.
  2. You need 50 ml of coconut milk.
  3. Prepare of Seasoning:.
  4. It's 2 cm of turmeric.
  5. You need 2 of garlic cloves.
  6. Prepare 3 of onions.
  7. Prepare 2 of candlenut.
  8. Prepare 1 tsp of coriander powder.
  9. Prepare of Additional:.
  10. Prepare 2 of lemongrass.
  11. Prepare 2 of lime leaves.
  12. It's 2 of bay leaves.
  13. Prepare 2 cm of galangal (smash).
  14. It's 2 cm of ginger (smash).
  15. Prepare of Fried onions.
  16. You need Pinch of pepper powder.
  17. It's Pinch of broth powder.
  18. It's of Green tomato (cut).
  19. Prepare of Green onion (chopped).

Soto Daging (Beef Soup with coconut milk and turmeric) step by step

  1. Boil beef until cooked, diced. Transfer beef into bowl, don't throw the water (beef broth)..
  2. Blend seasoning until smooth. Stir fry in a pan with 2 tbsp cooking oil. Pour the beef broth, add additional. Stir until boiling. Add beef..
  3. Put beef soup into bowl, put fried onions, green tomato, and green onion on top. You can eat this soup with rice..
